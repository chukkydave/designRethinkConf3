design rethink 2023

Design Rethink is a revolutionary conference that aims to build and push young creatives to become industry giants while contributing their unique quota to the growing creative economy. The conference is positioned to help creatives find their uniqueness, display, and market their works professionally, network, build career competence, develop new identities, and fight against plagiarism

What we seek to achieve:

  • The event aims to foster partnerships between organizations and top talents in order to create a collaborative environment.
  • It seeks to boost morale and encourage the embrace of new AI technology while emphasizing the ethical use of these technologies for improved productivity.
  • The event intends to grant recognition to creative individuals and enhance their business acumen.
  • It will provide a platform for creatives to showcase their talents and achievements.
  • The event aims to facilitate networking opportunities, allowing creatives to connect with potential collaborators and mentors.

design rethink 2021

The inaugural Design Rethink flagged off in November 2020 at the conclusion of Poe Hotspot's 7-month internship program, which trained a slew of young creatives to become professionals. The audience comprises intellectual jobs that are neither white nor blue-collar jobs, such as designers, programmers/software developers, embedded systems experts, writers and motion storytellers, digital marketers, and entertainers. There were over a hundred creatives and nine speakers, including Tola Alabi, Redona Dida, Abiodun Bada, Mooyo Okonta, Oche Kre8tive, Chidike Kizito, Kachi Eze, Poemack Emeaba and Ogbuji Miracle. This was focused to equip young creative enthusiasts with skills and an excellent mentality that will help them stand out in the saturated market.

The second edition of Design Rethink was held in September 2022 and was focused on the opportunities in the fast-developing creative economy, celebrating the creative role in different sectors of the economy, and preparing them for global relevance. Our Speakers at last year’s event were Tola Alabi, George Omoraro, Bolanle Banwo, Adedeji Owonibi, and Osaze Efe, they provided thought-provoking insights into creative growth, business management, and the emerging crypto technology markets.
